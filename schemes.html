Government Schemes

Explore beneficial agricultural schemes and subsidies for farmers

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Eligibility: All farmers owning cultivable land
₹6,000 per year in three installments
Duration: Till farming operations are active
Soil Health Card Scheme
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Purpose: Assess soil health and provide recommendations
Free soil testing and personalized recommendations
Coverage: 8 crore hectares
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Coverage: Crop failure due to natural calamities
Comprehensive insurance protection for farmers
Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi
Kisan Credit Card Scheme
Ministry of Finance
Purpose: Easy credit access for agricultural operations
Flexible credit lines up to ₹5 lakhs
Interest Rate: Concessional rates available
National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Focus: Sustainable farming practices and climate resilience
Technical support and financial assistance for adoption
Target: Improve productivity sustainably
Agriculture Infrastructure Fund
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Purpose: Build agricultural infrastructure
₹1 lakh crore funding available
Support: For post-harvest infrastructure
